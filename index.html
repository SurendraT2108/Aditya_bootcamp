About the Bootcamp

This bootcamp bridges the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements. Students work on real-world challenges, develop teamwork, and use tools applied in leading tech companies.

The core objective is to strengthen problem-solving skills and ensure students build practical, industry-oriented experience.

Bootcamp Highlights

  • Hands-on learning with real industry datasets
  • Team-based collaboration and execution
  • Guidance from industry mentors
  • Exposure to modern tools & frameworks
  • Real-world problem solving
  • Professional project presentation

Important Instructions for Participants

  1. Each team must have a Lead and 4–6 members.
  2. Enter accurate details — submissions sync directly to Google Sheets.
  3. Choose a problem statement that can be implemented practically.
  4. Final working demo must be presented on Project Day.
  5. Maintain discipline and follow mentor instructions.
